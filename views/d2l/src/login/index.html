FAQ

Is the program free?
Yes, the program is entirely free. We are a non-profit organization and we do not charge fees for our services. If you are pleased with our services, please consider supporting us with a donation!
What subjects can I find help with?
Our tutors can help you with English, science, social studies, and math; we invite you to look at our full list of subjects. If possible, please send us any material such as textbooks, homework, or notes prior to the tutoring session so our tutors can preview the material and give you a better understanding of the topics.
Can I work with the same tutor again?
Absolutely! We schedule multiple sessions with your tutor, even if it is your first time signing up.
